Our Story

Crafted with passion, poured with pure freshness

Ép Ép Now began as a rooftop juice pop-up, where we experimented with bold flavor pairings and honest ingredients. Today, we cold-press seasonal fruits, herbs, and botanicals into blends that energize your day without the sugar crash.

  • 1 Whole, local produce sourced from trusted farmers we know by name.
  • 2 Cold-pressed daily to lock in nutrients, aromas, and vibrant colors.
  • 3 Zero additives — just pure fruit, fresh herbs, and functional add-ons like chia, ginger, and spirulina.
